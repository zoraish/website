Mission & Vision

Mission

We are determined to support the needy with adequate care and protection. We aim to promote hope and well-being together, work in partnership to enable great lives and to offer a complete range of services and help to remove the social stigma from the live of deprived people in the society.

Vision

The primary aim of the Trust is to assist and improve the life of deprived people in the society providing them significant level of care and support.
