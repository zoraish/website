About us

For Everyone !

Our Journey working for the community began 9 years ago in New Zealand. Despite such varied histories, we have seen the same shared values that bring people together. We believe that every person has a right to live a better life. The primary aim to set up this charitable trust is to make a positive difference in people’s life. We provide culturally appropriate professional services to those in people. We are intent to make a difference to the world around us, in whatever ways we can. In doing so, we will pass on our values to the next generation, so that they can continue to engage in society around them this will enable them to lead an active life in the community. We truly understand that charitable purposes must be beneficial to the community that is why we have our focus on the following objectives;

  • The prevention or relief of poverty.
  • The advancement of education and health.
  • The advancement of religion, the care, support, and assistance of those with physical, mental, spiritual health care needs.
  • The advancement of social programs, teaching programs, the support of religious group which provide support and help to people in the community
  • The advancement of arts, heritage, culture, or science.
  • The care, support, and assistance of the disadvantaged or marginalized, support, and services to the victim of family violence.
  • The relief of those in need because of age, ill-health, disability, financial hardship, or other disadvantages.
